About Us

BOLD was founded by Camila Rivera and Mateo Cruz, a Puerto Rican couple with a passion for storytelling through photography. Inspired by the island’s energy and culture, they built BOLD to capture moments that are fearless, vibrant, and unforgettable. From intimate portraits to bold brand visuals, their work reflects the heart of Puerto Rico; and the soul behind every story. Together, Camila and Mateo bring heart, heritage, and a fearless eye to every frame; because at BOLD, your story deserves to be seen in its most powerful light.

Our Mission

To capture powerful, authentic moments that celebrate identity, emotion, and culture; through photography that’s fearless, vibrant, and deeply personal.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized creative studio rooted in Puerto Rican heritage, inspiring connection and storytelling through bold visual artistry.
